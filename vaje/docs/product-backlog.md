# Product Backlog – 3 Personas

## User Stories

---

### 1 – Luka
As Luka,  
I want to see all my transactions in one place,  
so that I have a clear overview of my finances.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Prikaže vse transakcije iz povezanega računa  
- Podatki se osvežijo vsaj 1x dnevno  

---

### 2 – Maja
As Maja,  
I want to set a dynamic monthly budget based on income,  
so that I can adjust spending depending on irregular earnings.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Sistem omogoča vnos variabilnega prihodka  
- Prilagoditev budgeta samodejno izračuna porabo  

---

### 3 – Jan
As Jan,  
I want to get weekly spending alerts,  
so that I don’t overspend on small impulzive nakupe.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Prejem obvestil vsaj 1× tedensko  
- Sistem opozori, ko je poraba nad 80 % tedenskega limita  

---

### 4 – Luka
As Luka,  
I want automatic categorization of expenses,  
so that I don’t have to manually track spending.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Vsaka transakcija dobi kategorijo  
- Natančnost ≥ 80 %  

---

### 5 – Maja
As Maja,  
I want to visualize my cash flow trends,  
so that I can plan for months with lower income.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Grafi za prihodke in odhodke  
- Prikaz vsaj zadnjih 6 mesecev  

---

### 6 – Jan
As Jan,  
I want to set saving goals for my trips,  
so that I stay motivated to save.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Cilj € + čas  
- Prikazan napredek  

---

### 7 – Luka
As Luka,  
I want to receive personalized AI tips for reducing expenses,  
so that I can improve my spending habits.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Vsaj 1 nasvet tedensko  
- Nasvet temelji na pretekli porabi  

---

### 8 – Maja
As Maja,  
I want notifications before I exceed my monthly budget,  
so that I can avoid financial stress.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Obvestilo pri 80 % in 100 % porabe  
- Push notifications delujejo na telefonu  

---

### 9 – Jan
As Jan,  
I want a simple dashboard for my weekly spending,  
so that I quickly understand my financial status.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Pregled ključnih podatkov < 5 minut  
- Grafični prikaz po kategorijah  

---

### 10 – Luka
As Luka,  
I want to track recurring subscriptions,  
so that I can cancel unnecessary services.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Sistem zazna ponavljajoče stroške  
- Možnost označiti kot "unsubscribed"  

---

### 11 – Maja
As Maja,  
I want to connect multiple bank accounts,  
so that I see full financial picture.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Povezava vsaj 2 bančnih računov  
- Podatki se združijo in vizualizirajo  

---

### 12 – Jan
As Jan,  
I want predicted spending for the month,  
so that I can plan better.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Napoved za ostanek meseca  
- Odstopanje < 20 %  

---

### 13 – Luka
As Luka,  
I want export functionality,  
so that I can analyze my data externally.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- CSV export  
- Prenos z enim klikom  

---

### 14 – Maja
As Maja,  
I want to set reminders for bill payments,  
so that I avoid late fees.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Uporabnik vnese datume računov  
- Sistem pošlje obvestilo 1 dan prej  

---

### 15 – Jan
As Jan,  
I want notifications for large transactions,  
so that I am aware of significant expenses.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Threshold nastavljiv (€)  
- Push notifications  

---

### 16 – Luka
As Luka,  
I want to compare my current spending to last month,  
so that I can see improvement.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Graf primerjave dveh mesecev  
- Prikaz % razlike  

---

### 17 – Maja
As Maja,  
I want weekly financial summaries,  
so that I can plan ahead efficiently.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Poročilo vsebuje prihodke, odhodke, prihranke  
- Dostopno preko aplikacije in emaila  

---

### 18 – Jan
As Jan,  
I want AI suggestions for cheaper alternatives,  
so that I can save money without major lifestyle changes.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Vsaj 1 predlog na teden  
- Predlogi temeljijo na dejanski porabi  

---

### 19 – Luka
As Luka,  
I want to set notifications for budget thresholds by category,  
so that I can control specific spending areas.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Nastavitev na vsako kategorijo  
- Obvestilo, ko dosežena meja  

---

### 20 – Maja
As Maja,  
I want an overview of monthly savings progress,  
so that I feel motivated to continue saving.  

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Graf napredka  
- Prikaz % doseženega cilja
